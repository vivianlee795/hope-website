Hope Mikaelson

The One And Only Tribrid

Hope Andrea Mikaelson was born on May 2, 2012. She is the daughter of the hybrid, Klaus Mikaelson, and the Crescent Wolf Pack alpha, Hayley Marshall. She attends the Salvatore Boarding School in Mystic Falls, Virginia. Since she has become the Tribrid, she has been struggling with the realities of her current world.

Hope comes from the Original family, who were the first vampires to walk the Earth. She was born from a witch bloodline, through her paternal grandmother, Esther, who was known as The Original Witch. Both of her parents are werewolves, so she inherited the werewolf curse, which she triggered in 2026. She recently completed her transition as a vampire. This recent change makes her the first and only Tribrid.

Hope is played by Danielle Rose Russell, who reprised her role from the Originals. She is one of the main female characters of the show. She is caring, loyal, and intelligent. She cares and loves with her whole heart, and it really shows in the show. She is my favorite character of all time.
